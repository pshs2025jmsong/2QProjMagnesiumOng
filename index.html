Geography of Asia



Asia is the largest continent in the whole world, population and area-wise. It boasts an area of 44.58 million square kilometers, which is roughly 30% of Earth's land area, and a population of 4.561 billion in 2018, which is roughly 60% of the world population. (Wikipedia, n.d.)


Landmarks and Landscapes of Asia



From skyscrapers to mountains to ruins to villages, Asia offers a lot to please our eyes and mind. Some of the most popular landmarks and landscapes are the following:


  • Angkor Wat, Cambodia - Temples from the Khmer Civilization.
  • Bali, Indonesia - Filled with stunning beaches and temples.
  • Dead Sea, Israel - The saltiest of waters.
  • Forbidden City, Beijing, China - Home of Chinese Emperors for Five Centuries.
  • Kuang Si Falls, Laos - A popular tourist spot outside the city.
  • Phuket Island, Thailand - The Best Beaches in the Country.
  • Singapore - Urban and modern tourist spot with marvelous architecure.
